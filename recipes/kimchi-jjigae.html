Kimchi Jjigae

Description

Kimchi Jjigae or Kimchi Stew is a popular Korean stew cusine. It is a hearty and spicy dish great for the cold weathers.

Ingredients

  • Kimchi, cut into bite sized pieces
  • Kimchi Brine
  • Pork Shoulder or Pork Belly
  • Bite sized tofu pieces
  • Green Onions
  • Sliced Onion
  • Kosher Salt
  • Sugar
  • Gochugaru(Korean hot pepper flakes)
  • Gochujang
  • Toasted Sesame Oil
  • Anchovy Stock (or Chicken or Beef Broth)

Steps

  1. Place kimchi and kimchi brine in a shallow pot. Add pork and onion.
  2. Slice green onions diagonally and place in the pot.
  3. Add salt, sugar, hot pepper flakes and gochujang. Drizzle sesame oil and add the stock.
  4. Cover and cook for 10mins over medium high heat.
  5. Open and mix the seasonings. Lay tofu over the top.
  6. Cover and cook another 10 to 15 minutes over medium heat.
  7. Chop green onion and put on top of stew. Remove from heat and serve with rice.
