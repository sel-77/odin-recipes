Upgraded Indomie

Description

Levled up recipe for the popular instant noodle brand - Indomie

Ingredients

  • Indomie Noodles, includes seasonings(black, orange, fat), powder, crispy shallots
  • Scallions
  • Bacon
  • Water
  • Egg
  • Sausage Ramen (slicked beef franks)

Steps

  1. Slice up scallions and bacon
  2. Start cooking the noodles from the Indomie package
  3. Cook the bacon with a little water to prevent splatters. Cook until 80% of fat is out and set aside the bacon
  4. With the left over oil from the bacon, cook the egg/s, sunny-side up
  5. Place all the packaged seasonings on a bowl except for the fat and shallots
  6. Pour the bacon oil used to cook the eggs onto the bowl and stir
  7. Toss in the cooked noodles and mix well
  8. Top with scallions, bacon, eggs, sausage ramen, and crispy shallots(from the Indomie package).
