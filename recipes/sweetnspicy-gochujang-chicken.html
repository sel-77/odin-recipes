Sweet and Spicy Gochujang Chicken

Description

Protein ba

Ingredients

  • Gochujang
  • Soy sauce
  • Mirin
  • Honey
  • Garlic
  • Sesame Oil
  • Ginger
  • Canola Oil
  • Chicken (skinless, chopped breast or things)
  • Garnishes (toasted sesame seeds and green onions)
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Stir together gochujang, soy sauce, sweet rice wine, honey, garlic, sesame oil, and ginger in a small bowl.
  2. Heat oil over medium-high in a wok or large skillet. Add half of the chicken to the wok; cook and stir over medium-high until chicken is no longer pink, about 5 mins.
  3. Remove chicken from the wok. Repeat with remaining chicken. Then return all cooked chicken to the wok.
  4. Stir sauce well. Add to the wok; cook and stir until sauce has thickened and is bubbly, about 3 mins.
  5. Top with the garnishes and serve with rice.
